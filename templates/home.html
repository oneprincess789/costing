CostIt was created in an effort to move the apparel costing process away from spreadsheets and online.
CostIt not only allows customers to save their product history into a database, it makes the costing experience seamless,
more accurate and simpler without sacrificing the flexibility that has drawn the industry to spreadsheet documents in the past.
